What We Do

Energy that knows
who's using it.

In most shared residential buildings, energy is tracked at the unit level — but never at the individual level. Lights stay on. ACs run all night. And no one takes responsibility because no one sees the cost.

Aware+ changes this. Our system identifies each resident through facial recognition and attributes their exact energy and water usage in real time — displayed in Dirhams on smart panels and in a personal mobile app.

The System

Three steps to
smarter living.

01

Sense

Facial recognition cameras and smart meters identify each resident and measure their individual energy and water usage across shared spaces.

02

Process

All data is processed locally on edge devices. No video footage is stored. Only encrypted biometric templates — zero cloud exposure of personal data.

03

Act

Residents see their usage in Dirhams on smart panels and in the app. Real-time nudges, rewards, and insights turn awareness into lasting behavioral change.
