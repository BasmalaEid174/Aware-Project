What We Do

Energy that knows
who's using it.

In shared residential buildings, energy is tracked at the unit level — but never at the individual level. Lights stay on. ACs run all night. No one takes responsibility because no one sees the real cost.

Aware+ changes this. Our system identifies each resident through facial recognition and attributes their exact energy and water usage in real time — displayed in Dirhams on smart panels and in the Aware+ mobile app. The result: awareness that actually changes behavior.

Our Vision

A future where every kilowatt-hour is owned, understood, and intentional. We envision UAE communities where sustainable living isn't a sacrifice — it's the natural outcome of knowing your numbers. Aligned with the UAE Energy Strategy 2050 and its 42–45% efficiency improvement targets.

🎯

Our Mission

To give residents real individual energy accountability — and give developers the ESG data, green certifications (LEED, Estidama), and premium market positioning they need to build the next generation of high-value, sustainable homes across the UAE.

The System

Three steps to
smarter living.

01

Sense

Facial recognition cameras and smart energy meters identify each resident and measure their individual electricity and water usage in real time across every shared space in the building.

02

Process

All data is processed locally on edge devices. No video footage is ever stored — only encrypted biometric templates. No cloud exposure. Fully secure IoT communication at every step.

03

Act

Residents see their usage in Dirhams on in-unit smart panels and in the Aware+ app. A leaderboard, daily tips, and real rewards turn real-time awareness into lasting behavioral change.
